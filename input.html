GENERAL FQAs

Here are some general questions that young traders
or aspiring traders often ask their mentor or a leader, along with sample answers:




How did you start trading and what kept you going?

I started with a small account and a lot of curiosity. What kept me going was the vision of financial freedom and the discipline to learn from losses instead of giving up.

What is the most important skill in trading?

Discipline. You can have the best strategy, but if you lack discipline, you'll still lose money. Risk management and emotional control are also vital.

How do I handle losses emotionally?

Accept that losses are part of the game. Focus on the bigger picture — consistent growth over time. Always analyze your losses to learn from them.

What mistakes did you make as a beginner?

Overtrading, revenge trading, using high leverage, and not having a plan. These are common — that’s why mentorship matters.
